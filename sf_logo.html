Usage Guidelines
Use Icon Mark alone
For app icons, favicons, small avatars, and square crops at 32 px or above.
Wordmark on headers
Use the horizontal wordmark in app top-bars, landing pages, and slide headers.
On Dark variant
Default for all dark-theme screens, splash pages, and dark presentation slides.
On Orange variant
Use only on brand-orange backgrounds, CTAs, and marketing banners.
Don't recolour
Never change the X stroke colour or ECG pulse colour outside these four approved variants.
Minimum clear-space
Always maintain at least 1× the icon height as padding around any logo variant.
